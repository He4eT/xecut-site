Hackspace

{% include 'parts/hero.njk' %}

Xecut is a community-run hackerspace where makers, coders, and curious minds team up to learn, build, and share. We’ve got tools, equipment, and knowledge — whether you’re just starting out or already deep into your projects.

Below you can find out how to access the hackspace, how to contact us, and how you can support us.

Access

We’re based in Belgrade. You’re welcome to visit during events or by arrangement — just drop us a message in the chat.

Anyone is welcome to give a talk, run a workshop, or host a meetup.
